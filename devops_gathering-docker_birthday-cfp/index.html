Fourth Docker Birthday Talks at Bochum

Dear Friends and Speakers of Docker!

Inspire the people: though the first edition of DevOps Gathering Conference hasn’t even started yet, we are preparing the first round – Docker Birthday Talks in Bochum in March.

Those, who already have celebrated a Docker birthday, are very welcome. The others, who haven’t heard of this, please come and lets dance. The DevOps Gathering Conference format gives you the chance to stay in touch with other people that like cooperation and infrastructure technology. Technical topics have a home as well topics on innovative company culture.

In any case, we are looking forward to proposals with practical relevance – ideally based on project experiences or real technical inspiration.

As usual, we would like to invite you to submit your proposals for sessions and workshops for the March conference considering the following tracks:

  • Docker Orchestration
  • Docker Security
  • Docker Networking
  • Docker for Microservices
  • Docker at Windows or ARM
  • Docker for Java or Go Developer
  • Stateful Container
  • Provisioning
  • Continuous Integration and Delivery
  • Cloud
  • Logging & Monitoring
  • Container Technologies

Date: 24 – 25 March, 2017

Location: GData Campus Bochum

Deadline for submissions: January 23, 2017

You can offer us 45 minutes talks or 10 minutes lightning stories or tech speeches.

At 25.3 we plan a Docker Expert OpenSpace and you can discuss the hottest Docker container or ecosystem topics.

Submit your papers here: call4papers@bee42.com

We are looking forward to your comprehensive feedback as well as your questions, suggestions and discussions.

We wish you a peaceful Docker winter! We hope you are be(e) prepared for spring birthday party …

The bee42 Conference Crew
