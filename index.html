Mission Statement

As DevOps company, we combine the most talented people to help you establishing vertical solutions based on new technologies and collaborations.

The devOps company bee42 solutions gmbh in Bochum gives your life some more sense and works to find perfect solutions. We provide an unique infrastructure solution based on the Docker ecosystem for your need to build and operate your IT. We love to build infrastructure, implement software, measure systems and always look for the next enhancement. In order to achieve these goals, the bee42 works with effective and efficient methods and reflects their actions. Our workflow is carefully considered and almost perfectly organized. We make use of agile methods, reduce processes, live DevOps and create simple value . We offer trainings, workshops, labs and consulting to share our knowledge in a lean way. The crew of the bee42 always works like a busy bee, to provide the solutions in time and with high quality.
