4th Docker Birthday Party in the Ruhr Area

We love to use docker in our daily business, so there is no execuse not celebrate the 4th Docker Birthday! After some ideas about how we can make this event real, we asked our friends from Zalando and GData to do this with us. :-)

Our Docker Birthday #4 was celebrated at Zalando on the 16th March 2017. We brought both Community Groups from Bochum and Dortmund together to celebrate this event. To celebrate together we warmly welcomed newcomers and the already known docker friends! We provided Docker labs and courses to learn about Docker in a fun and inviting way.

We had hands-on with self-paced tutorials for every experience level from absolute beginners to professionals (with focus on beginners to intermediate).

The agenda for the Docker Birthday Party was as the following:

  • 18:00 - 18:30: Come together for dinner (Pizza & Drinks)
  • 18:30 - 18:45: Salutation and introductory words
  • 18:45 - 20:45: Mentoring sessions
  • 20:45 - 22:00: Celebration with drinks and a very special cake

We had a little schedule to finish all things we want to do. Our Meetup started with Pizza and Drink to get strong enough for all problems that we wanted to solve. After this Peter Roßssbach, our Docker expert, held an short introduction about Docker and the Docker Birthday courses.

With ending of the introduction all participants built small groups to handled the courses in a timeframe of 2 Hours. In some groups there were dedicated Mentors for the courses, because Mentors likes as well to learn new stuff and take challenges :) . And the other groups could get help from Mentors On-Demand or Mentors ask them if they can help on the problems.

After our timeframe ended, we missed some important step: The Docker Birthday Cake - The most important part of the evening!

It looked so small but trust us - the cake was 20 centimeters high, 20 kg heavy and it was filled with raspberry (pi, hihi) creme and and chocolate!!

After our stomach hurted from the delicious cake and the little sugar overdose, we took our chance make a selfie with some people:

Next year we want to repeat this nice event. We hope that you can join us as well to celebrate this day! :-)
