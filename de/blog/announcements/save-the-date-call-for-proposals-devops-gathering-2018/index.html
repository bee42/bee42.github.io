Call for Papers and Proposals

The next DevOps Gathering is now in preparation and we are looking for speakers, workshops and talks!

The DevOps Gathering is an international conference located in Bochum - surrounded by the metropolitan area of the Ruhr Valley. This melting pot of past times industrial revolutions is nowadays a hot spot for Germany’s IT industry - a perfect place to discuss the next big revolution of our industry, the DevOps revolution. Let’s discuss the hot topics inside the DevOps community and start talking about Docker, Kubernetes, Microservices, Clouds and everything else regarding DevOps.

We are hosting a non-profit conference striving for high-quality content and providing a platform for experienced international speakers at the G DATA Campus in the heart of Bochum.

Everyone is invited to join to discuss the hottest topics of the DevOps development with its impact on the digital transformation. Join our journey and cooperate on infrastructure technology and innovative solutions to revolutionize the software industry.

We are looking forward to proposals with practical relevance – ideally based on project experiences or real technical inspiration.

  • Date: February 19 - 21, 2018
  • Location: G Data Campus in Bochum, Germany
  • Start for submissions: July 1, 2017
  • Deadline for submissions: September 6, 2017

As usual, we’d like to invite you to submit your proposals for sessions in February. We will focus on the following topics, but are always open for surprises with new inspiring talks:

You can offer us 30 minutes talks or 10 minutes lightning stories or tech speeches

  • Container Standards
  • Container Orchestration (Docker Swarm, Kubernetes, Mesos, Rancher …)
  • Container Security
  • Container Networking
  • Container for Microservices
  • Docker on Windows
  • Docker and Kubernetes on ARM and IoT devices
  • Container for Developers (Java, Go, Ruby, Javascript, PHP, …)
  • Stateful Container
  • Provisioning
  • Continuous Integration and Delivery
  • GitLab/CI
  • Linuxkit
  • Infrakit
  • Loadbalancing (Traefik, kong, HAProxy, Nginx)
  • Cloud
  • Logging & Monitoring (Graylog, ELK, Prometheus, Grafana, …)
  • Container Technologies
  • Microservice based Front-End Development
  • Provisioning

Submit here: https://www.papercall.io/devops-gathering-2018

If you have any questions please contact us via e-mail: call4papers@bee42.com
